Common Sleep Disorders

Despite our efforts to get about 8 hours of sleep each night, sleep disorders can disrupt the amount and/or quality of sleep we get. The four most common sleep disorders are Insomnia, Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA), Periodic Limb Movement (PLM) Disorder, and Restless Leg Syndrome (RLS).

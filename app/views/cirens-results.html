Your CIRENS score is calculated by subtracting your answer to item #1 from your answer to item #2.

CIRENS scores range from -4 to 4:

Morning Energy Type - score of -4 to -2
Neither Energy Type = score of -1 to +1
Evening Energy Type = score of +2 to +4

Your CIRENS score is generally related to your preferred bedtimes and wake-up times. For example, lower CIRENS scores often correlate with earlier preferred wake-up times, and higher CIRENS scores often correlate with later preferred wake-up times. Your CIRENS score may change (usually become lower or more morning-preferring) as you get older.

What's My Charge?

This tool provides an estimate of mental speed based on sleep amounts, time of day, and caffeine consumed.

The needle on the gauge points to predicted mental speed at the time and date shown. Use the slide bar below the gauge to scroll forward by 2 days or backward by 2 days and see how mental speed varies.

To see how different sleep amounts or caffeine affects mental speed, click the button below the gauge to input your personal sleep schedule or caffeine intake.

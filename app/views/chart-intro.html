What's My Charge Introduction

What's My Charge allows you to see how your sleep amounts and caffeine use affect a prediction of your reaction time. What's My Charge is pre-loaded with a reaction time prediction across 7 days, based on 8 hours of sleep (23:00 to 07:00) each night. 7-9 hours per night is the medically recommended sleep amount to maintain performance.

You can edit the pre-loaded sleep schedule to match when you plan to sleep (for example, based on an upcoming work schedule). What's My Charge will then display your predicted reaction time. At points where your predicted reaction time slows below the green zone, you can add a caffeine event (for example, drinking a 12-oz cola) to see how much reaction time improvement you could expect from consuming that amount of caffeine.

Planning your sleep and caffeine consumption using What's My Charge predictions will help you stay fully charged and on top of your game.

What's My Charge predicts an average person - your actual reaction times might be faster or slower than those predictions.

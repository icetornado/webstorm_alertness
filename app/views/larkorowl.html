Lark or Owl?

Do you prefer waking up early? Or are you more alert at night? Your preference for the morning or the evening (or somewhere in between) is called your chronotype. People who prefer waking up early and performing activities earlier in the day are called "Larks." People who prefer waking up closer to noon and staying up late at night are called "Owls."

  • To get a quick Chronotype score, take a short (2-item) Circadian Energy Scale. This scale takes about 1 minute to complete.

    • Complete the 2-item Circadian Energy Scale

  • To answer more detailed questions about your chronotype, take the longer 19-item Morningness-Eveningness questionnaire. The 19-item questionnaire takes about 5 minutes to complete.

    • Complete the 19-item Morningness-Eveningness Questionnaire
